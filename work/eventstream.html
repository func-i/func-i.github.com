Functional Imperative

An immersive experience for broadcast viewers. Built to be responsive from day one.

Eventstream is a premium webcasting and live streaming service. Streaming a live event to thousands of participants requires a lengthy and complex setup and is prohibitively expensive and difficult for most users. Hoping to expand the market beyond the few large corporations employ professional streaming services to smaller enterprises and individuals, Eventstream approached Functional Imperative to design and build its next generation self-service live streaming tool, eventually called Kastio.

Kastio focuses on simplifying initial setup and configuration, while providing the advanced user with enough options to get the most out of the system.

Complete features

  • Custom responsive landing pages
  • Pay-walls
  • Multiple camera angles
  • Powerpoint and Keynote sharing
  • Surveys and quizzes
  • Moderated chat
  • Flickr and Twitter integration
  • Embeddable iFrames
  • Custom analytics

Working with our Toronto-based design partners, Half Hunter, Functional Imperative designed and built an immersive experience for broadcast viewers that automatically focuses on the content (video stream, powerpoint, twitter, etc.) that the viewer wants to watch when they want to watch it. Built to be responsive from day one, Kastio works across devices from mobile phones and tablets to extra-large screens, like televisions.

Pushing the live streaming bounderies and to be at the forefront of the market

Kastio asked Functional Imperative to push the boundary between web and traditional desktop applications across a host of devices. To deliver, Functional Imperative relied upon and iterated upon a number of emerging front-end technologies like AngularJS, WebSockets and HTML5 video. On the backend, the core application was built in Ruby on Rails with chat and analytics built in Node.js.

Through a mixture of great design and new technologies, Functional Imperative was able to deliver a product to Kastio that could catapult them to the forefront of the live streaming market.
