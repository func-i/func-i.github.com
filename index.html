Our Mission...

We are a boutique web design & development agency based in Toronto. Our focus on launching early and iterating often helps entrepreneurs and established businesses alike validate their ideas and facilitates the transition from idea, through prototype, all the way to viral adoption.

SocialStreet

SocialStreet facilitates real world interactions by helping you find things to do with the people around you.

Entirely designed and built by FunctionalImperative, SocialStreet is an AJAX heavy user-experience that puts the focus of an inherently location-based application right where it should be.

www.socialstreet.com

Span6

Span6 is a cutting-edge Online Marketing & Sales platform that enables advertisers to easily channel traffic from affiliate campaigns, convert visitors into leads and track performance through the entire process.

Built from the ground up by FunctionalImperative, the work continues as Span6 grows into its market leading position.

www.span6.com

Hammerati

uKnowa originally hired Fi to design the API for their Hammerati product. This partnership was quickly expanded to their entire process as they were able to realize dramatic improvements in development velocity.

With strong momentum and Fi's help, uKnowa is poised to disrupt the entire construction industry.

www.hammerati.com
