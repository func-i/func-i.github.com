What is FunctionalImperative?

We are a boutique web design & development agency based in Toronto. Our focus on launching early and iterating often helps entrepreneurs and established businesses alike validate their ideas and facilitates the transition from idea, through prototype, all the way to viral adoption.

Interesting projects and the people who made it happen

SocialStreet facilitates real world interactions by helping you find things to do with the people around you. Entirely designed and built by FunctionalImperative, SocialStreet is an AJAX heavy user-experience that put the focus of an inherently location-based application right where it should be.

Span6 is a cutting-edge Online Marketing & Sales platform that enables advertisers to easily channel traffic from affiliate campaigns, convert visitors into leads and track performance through the entire process. Built from the ground up by FunctionalImperative, the work continues as Span6 grows into its market leading position.

uKnowa originally hired FunctionalImperative to design the API for their Hammerati product. This partnership was quickly expanded to their entire process as they were able to realize dramatic improvements in development velocity. With strong momentum and Func-i's help, uKnowa is poised to disrupt the entire construction industry.

Who is FunctionalImperative?

Josh Borts

Co-Founder and CEO, Josh grew up in Montreal and studied computer engineering at Waterloo. His nomadic, adventurous & inquisitive nature has led him to work, live and travel around the world.

Having moved back to Canada at the start of 2011, he started FunctionalImperative to work with amazing businesses who want to change the world.

Khurram Virani

Co-Founder & Lead Developer, Khurram has worked in web development for most of the past decade. He brings the experience and expertise necessary to deliver the amazing experiences that today's web products demand.

A true romantic, he likes long walks on the beach, sunsets and emoticons. When not at work, he can be found working from home.
