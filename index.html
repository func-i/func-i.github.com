Creativity - We're all over the place:

Our creativity is built on the diversity of our team's backgrounds. Functional Imperative includes former high speed traders and wanna-be-rock-and-roll-stars; reformed intellectual property lawyers and Korean-taco restauranteurs; we have worked in international aid and international criminal law, and, of course, we are entrepreneurs. We have also lived and worked in dozens of countries on six continents. Unique solutions start with our unique way of thinking. And whether in finance or health, law or real-estate, we speak your language.

Got skills?

Together we have decades of coding experience across multiple verticals. We have resisted the urge to be generalists, and have focused our team's immense talent on a fixed technology stack. This expertise allows us to delve deeper and be trend setters, not followers. While we are focused on technology, our team also includes some of Canada's brightest talents in UX/UI and design. These team members mean we can not only make any idea an online reality but we can also make it frictionless and beautiful too.

Our capabilities include:

  • Business analysis and information architecture
  • Interactive web development
  • Responsive design and user experience
  • Native and HTML5 mobile development
  • Social media integration and deployment
  • Cloud-based infrastructure management

An in-person relationship

While we have immense respect for the incredible wealth of talent overseas, we feel there is nothing like a long chat over strong coffee or a scotch to ensure that we build what you want the way you want it built. Because great solutions begin with a thorough understanding of the problem, we start by asking a lot of questions. In response, we often feel compelled to provide a blunt and transparent perspective on your business model. This assessment provides a basis for us to explore, together, whether and how Functional Imperative's software and design services can work for you. This process is meant to build trust, which is fundamental to any successful relationship and something that can never be off-shored.

Popup Archive is a lightweight web-based system that makes audio archives searchable.

Launched at SXSW 2013, Popup tasked Fi with the development of an interactive story to introduce prospective clients. Building upon new web technologies like the Web Audio API and SVG, Fi delivered an experience as cutting edge as the archive itself.

www.PopupArchive.org

TFC - On The Road

A community based partnership between the Toronto Football Club and Rogers Communications that is designed to connect, grow and celebrate soccer participation from the grass roots level up.

Fi was tasked with building an iPad data capture and analysis tool that leverages HTML5 local storage to reliably upload data for later analysis despite poor internet connectivity.

www.TorontoFC.ca/on-the-road

Fi was hired to continue development of WineAlign's iOS and Android mobile applications, both of which are available for download in the respective app stores.
