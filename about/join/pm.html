Functional Imperative

Project Manager

Us

We build beautiful mobile apps, complex websites, and enterprise software. Founded by one of Canada's top Ruby on Rails software developers, a high frequency algorithmic trading expert, and an international criminal and intellectual property lawyer, we want to use software to build a better society. We develop this custom software for a wide variety of clients including in the legal, healthcare, real estate, startup and social impact verticals.

You

Detail-obsessed, empathetic, outgoing, and passionate about technology. You're excited to help keep clients and our development team happy. You want autonomy to make things better.

Primary Duties

  • Refine effective, scalable, agile project management processes for now and our future.
  • Be our representative for clients throughout the project lifecycle, including sprint planning, updates, demos.
  • Communicate transparently with clients and development team to maintain trust and encourage an open environment.
  • Ensure development follows our processes, including pull requests, code reviews, QA.
  • Manage project schedules, cost, documents.
  • Contribute to decisions regarding scoping, release schedules, business analysis.
  • Share the occassional dog .gif in Slack.
